Product Info

MADE WITH TITAN

iPhone 15 Pro has a strong and lightweight design in aerospace-grade titanium, with textured, matte glass on the back. In addition, the front has Ceramic Shield, which is stronger than the glass on any smartphone. And it is splash, water and dust resistant.

ADVANCED SCREEN

The 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion increases the screen frequency to 120 Hz when you need extreme graphics performance. Dynamic Island causes real-time alerts and updates to bubble up. And with Always On Screen, the locked screen is always visible, so you don't have to tap it to find out what's going on.

A17 PROCESSOR CHIP

A Pro-class GPU makes mobile games extremely immersive - with incredible detail in the environments and more realistic characters. The A17 Pro is incredibly efficient and helps ensure that the battery can also last all day

ADVANCED PRO-CAMERA SYSTEM

Seven Pro lenses give you fantastic flexibility in choosing the scene. Take super high-resolution photos with more colors and details using the 48 MP main camera. And take sharp close-ups from further away with the 3x telephoto camera on the iPhone 15 Pro.

ADJUSTABLE FUNCTION SIDE BUTTON

The action button takes you directly to your favorite function. Just choose which one, such as Silent Mode, Camera, Voice Recording, Shortcuts and more. Then just hold down the button to open it.

PRO CONNECTION

With the new USB C connection, you can charge your Mac or iPad with the same cable you use to charge your iPhone 15 Pro. With USB 3 you get much faster data transfer.4 And with Wi-Fi 6E you can download files up to twice as fast.

IMPORTANT SECURITY FUNCTIONS

With Accident Detection, your iPhone can detect a serious car accident and call for help when you can't.

MADE TO MAKE A DIFFERENCE

iPhone has privacy features that ensure you have full control over your data. It is made from recycled materials to minimize the environmental impact. And it has built-in features that make it possible for anyone to use an iPhone.

COMES WITH APPLE CARE WARRANTY

Every iPhone comes with a one-year limited warranty and up to 90 days of free technical support. Choose AppleCare+ or AppleCare+ with theft and loss coverage to be even safer.

LEGAL INFORMATION

iPhone 15, iPhone 15 Plus, iPhone 15 Pro and iPhone 15 Pro Max have been tested under controlled laboratory conditions and are splash, water and dust resistant with a rating of IP68 according to IEC standard 60529 (maximum depth of 6 meters for up to 30 minutes). Splash, water and dust resistance are not permanent conditions. Durability can be weakened as a result of normal wear and tear. Do not charge a wet iPhone. The user manual describes how to clean and dry it. The warranty does not cover damage caused by liquid.

The screen has rounded corners. Measured as a rectangle, the screen is 6.12 inches (iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15) or 6.69 inches (iPhone 15 Pro Max, iPhone 15 Plus) diagonally. The visible screen area is smaller.

Battery life varies depending on use and configuration. You can find more information at apple.com/no/batteries.

USB 3 cable with 10 Gbps required for up to 20x faster transfer on iPhone 15 Pro models.

Wi Fi 6E is available in countries where this technology is supported.

iPhone 15 and iPhone 15 Pro can register a serious car accident and call for help. Requires mobile network or Wi-Fi calling.
